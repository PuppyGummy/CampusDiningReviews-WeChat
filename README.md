<h1>Campus Dining Reviews</h1>
<p>This is a WeChat applet developed within 48 hours, during the <a href="https://www.hackpku.com/">Hackathon</a> held by Peking University.</p>
<br>
<div style="text-align:center;">
<img height="600px" src="https://s2.loli.net/2022/01/25/ZD7EhT9xmAK8IFa.png">
<br>
</div>
<p>Through the application, students in the university can review and rate the dishes in the cafeteria, which helps them to choose what to eat during mealtime.</p>
<br>
<div style="text-align:center;">
<img height="600px" src="https://s2.loli.net/2022/01/25/bkAHxD4dr7L3QXm.png">
<p style="color:DarkGrey">reviewing dishes</p>
</div>
<br>
<p>
It also allows students to upload dishes that are not included in the database, and give detailed information like location, price, calories, flavor, and average waiting time for meals.
</p>
<br>
<div style="text-align:center;">
<img height="600px" src="https://s2.loli.net/2022/01/25/I5MO6ZWhtqRLK8n.png">
